The Sacred Sakura

The climb to the Grand Narukami Shrine is a journey of purification. Thousands of vibrant red Torii gates line the winding stairs of Mount Yougou, each one a silent witness to the prayers of countless souls. As the air grows thin and the scent of Sakura blossoms fills the wind, the noise of the world below fades into the hum of sacred thunder.

Since ancient times, the shrine has stood as the ultimate shield against the "Sacred Sakura Cleansing." The roots of the divine tree reach deep into the earth, drawing out the filth and miasma that plague the land. It is here, under the moon's silver glow, that the shrine maidens perform their dances to ensure the eternity of Narukami's grace.
